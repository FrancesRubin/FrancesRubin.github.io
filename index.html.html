Frances Rubin

Visual Designer

Frances is a seasoned graphic designer with more than ten years of experience in the field. She has worked with a diverse array of clients from international organizations, global companies and start-ups in various industries. She takes personal care of projects from beginning to end making sure the highest standards are met while exceeding client expectations. She is responsible for the conceptualization, design and production of reports, publications, presentations, conference materials, marketing collateral, websites, and portals. Francesâ€™ versatility and resourcefulness make her a precious asset to any team.

She earned her BA in graphic design from the Montemar Graphic Design School; now living in the Washington DC area, she continues to expand her career by attending webinars about User Experience and data visualization. In summer of 2015 Frances received her User Experience Design certification from Nielsen Norman Consulting, and a certification in Data Visualization from The New York University. Now, Frances is working and expanding her portfolio as a Visual designer. She is currently enrolled in the HTML, CSS Web Design Circuit at General Assembly.
